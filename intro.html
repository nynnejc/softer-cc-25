Nynne Just Christoffersen is a Copenhagen based software developer with a decade of industry experience from places such as SoundCloud and LEGO. Throughout her career, she has been teaching and building communities for softer kinds of people into tech. She is a lecturer in Computer Science at the Copenhagen School of Design and Technology (KEA), a member of Cybernauterne tech collective and, sporadically, a blogger. This is the story of how she started learning how to code. For softer, May 2025.
